Privacy Policy for NaviVortex At NaviVortex, we value your privacy. This policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it. Data Collection: We collect basic profile information (Name, Email) only when you sign in via Google Firebase. This data is used solely to provide "Cloud Save" features for your flight missions. Cookies and Web Beacons: NaviVortex uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and to record user-specific information on which pages the user accesses or visits. Google AdSense: We use third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Log Files: Like many other websites, NaviVortex makes use of log files to analyze trends and administer the site. IP addresses are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
