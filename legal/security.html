Security Statement

Your data security is our top priority.

Authentication

We use Google Firebase Authentication. This means we never see or store your passwords on our servers. All login processes are handled directly by Google's secure infrastructure.

Data Encryption

All data transmission between your browser and our cloud servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS (HTTPS) protocols.

Cloud Storage

Your mission data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, benefiting from Google's world-class data center security and redundancy.
