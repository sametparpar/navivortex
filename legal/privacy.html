Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At NaviVortex, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our application and how it is used.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services. When you sign in via Google Firebase Authentication, we may receive basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this data solely to authenticate your identity and provide cloud synchronization for your flight missions.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We use cookies to enhance your user experience. Google AdSense, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on NaviVortex. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Flight & Mission Data

The coordinates, waypoints, and mission parameters you create are stored securely on Google Cloud (Firestore). This data is private to your account unless you explicitly choose to share it. We do not sell, trade, or rent your flight data to third parties.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmission happens over secure SSL/TLS (HTTPS) channels. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
