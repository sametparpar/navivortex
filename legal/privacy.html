Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At NaviVortex, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect and use data.

1. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you sign in via Google Firebase. This may include your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this data solely to provide cloud synchronization for your flight missions.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Google AdSense, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site based on your prior visits to NaviVortex or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Flight Data

Coordinates and mission parameters you create are stored on Google Cloud (Firebase). We do not sell or share this data with third parties. You have the right to delete your saved missions at any time via the application interface.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
