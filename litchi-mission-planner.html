How to Export Waypoints to Litchi?

  1. Click the Launch Planner button above to open the NaviVortex 3D map.
  2. Select Electric Drone from the Vehicle Profile menu.
  3. Click on the map to drop waypoints, or use the Smart Tools (Grid) to generate a search pattern.
  4. Review your flight path, elevation profile, and estimated time en route (ETE).
  5. Open the Mission Actions menu and click üì• EXPORT CSV.
  6. Log in to the Litchi Mission Hub website and import your downloaded CSV file!
